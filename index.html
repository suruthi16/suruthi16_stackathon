Our Main Motto

We mainly focuses on the benifit and upliftment of the farmers. We sell the products directly from farmer to the customer. Our motive is to provide fresh and healthy fruits and vegetables right from the farm to the customer. The process of selling the vegetables and fruits via three different retailers and finally reaching out to the customer is highly toxic as they add various chemicals to preserve the nature of the fruit. V for U provides delivery within one day right from the farm itself. Though our delivery is within a day,we don't add any chemicals.You can get a fresh product from us.

01

Chemical free product

We supply the required product directly from the farm and no extra chemicals are added. Hence the grains, vegetables and fruits supplied by us is totally healthy and safe.

02

Transportation

We provide free doorstep delivery for the customer, so that there is no need of travelling a long distance. It also save time and money for the customer.

03

Affordable

Since we act as a bridge between farmers and customer the cost price for the customer decreases and the selling price for the farmer will increase, which results in increasing of profit percent for both farmer and customer.

Gamified Platform

Gamified Platform


This is a unique platform, in which the customer can gain profit with fun. Each customer will be provided with special coins, when they purchased some product from us. With this coins they can do online farming and plant some crops of their wish.In this they will gain a new experience of farming. After farming they can take the whole product which they cultivated for free. If the customers are in lack of coins, they may not able to water their plants. So, if they want some more coins they should purchase some products from us.
